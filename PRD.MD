# Product Requirements Document: SnapBroker SaaS

## Vision
To provide a zero-cost business operating system for freelance photographers while acting as a trusted marketplace/broker for clients to discover and book talent.

## Problem Statement
Photographers face high costs for portfolio hosting and struggle with manual client management. Clients lack a centralized, verified platform to find photographers based on specific dates and locations.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Search & Discovery:** Filter by Location, Category, and Real-time availability.
2. **Business Suite:** Digital Quotations (PDF), E-Contracts, and Expense Tracking.
3. **Smart Delivery:** 48-hour "Flash" proofing with automated cloud deletion.
4. **Broker Dashboard:** Admin tools to verify photographers and mediate disputes.

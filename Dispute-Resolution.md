# Dispute Resolution & Broker Policy

## 1. The Broker's Authority
As the platform owner (Admin), SnapBroker maintains the right to mediate between Photographers and Clients to ensure a fair marketplace.

## 2. Mediation Triggers
- **Non-Delivery:** If a photographer fails to upload the 48-hour proofing gallery within the agreed timeline.
- **Payment Disputes:** If a client claims to have paid the balance, but the photographer has not unlocked the High-Res link.

## 3. Technical Resolution Tools
- **Admin Override:** The Admin can manually set `is_drive_unlocked = 1` in the database if proof of payment is provided.
- **Account Suspension:** Photographers with a "Dispute Rate" higher than 10% will be automatically flagged for review and potential de-listing.
